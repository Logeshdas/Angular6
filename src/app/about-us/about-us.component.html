About Us















Sharjah Asset Management Holding (SAMH), the investment arm of the government of Sharjah, and Apleona HSG Facility Management, a German leading company in FM and Technical Services have founded a joint venture in 2016 to provide various technical related services to all types of properties within the public and private sectors in the Emirate of Sharjah.

The scope of the JV includes Integrated Facility Management, Small and Medium construction project management, HSE related scopes such as Fire Prevention strategies, HSE inspection and Training, Security Systems integration, landscaping, etc.

Vision

To be among the best facility management service providers in the Middle East, influencing the over all FM industry in high quality and modern practices.

Mission

Utilizing the latest worldwide technologies and the best human resources to achieve sustainable business and an enviable reputation in the local and regional markets.
