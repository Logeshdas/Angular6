Description

SALAMA is a high-level training center established as a collaboration between Sharjah Prevention and Safety Authority (SPSA) and SANED, to serve the various sectors in the Emirate of Sharjah, providing specialized training programs in the prevention and safety of workers within the private and public sectors.

The training center will enable citizens to centrally receive, process, manage and report on “labor training on prevention and safety”.

Objectives

Enhance the quality of life in the Emirate of Sharjah by educating the labor force on the highest internationally recognized standards of protection and safety in the workplace. The mission is a Zero Harm Environment.

Technical Information

The user friendly SPSA Mobile App and SALAMA online registration portal make it easy for the Company Human Resources departments to register their companies, enlist the employees they wish to train, and track their company compliance.

Commercial Information

For a fixed fee, the employee will receive a one-day, in-person training taught by an instructor that is accredited by the Government of Sharjah Prevention and Safety Authority (SPSA). They will receive a certificate of completion upon passing the exam which will be administered the day of the training.
