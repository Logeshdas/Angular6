Salama




















Description

SALAMA is a first of its kind Training Centre aiming to serve various segments in the Emirate of Sharjah, providing specialized training programs in prevention and safety of workers within private and public sectors. SALAMA offers basic courses at the beginning, which will then develop to provide advanced courses and training programs to align with the requirements in different fields. This idea was adopted by the Sharjah Prevention and Safety Authority in collaboration with SANED to help reducing injuries and incidents due to lack of knowledge of basic safety precautions.
