Aman















Description

To ensure fast response to fire emergencies, SPSA partnered with SANED have initiated “AMAN” project. AMAN, aims to protect lives and properties by establishing a safe and reliable connection between all firefighting systems in all properties in Sharjah, with the central monitoring station that is continuously monitored to identify and respond to emergencies instantly ensuring the safety of people and assets.

Objectives

Enhance the quality of life in the Emirate of Sharjah by applying the highest internationally recognized standards to ensure the protection and safety of individuals and properties. The mission is a Zero Harm Environment.

Technical Information

The 24/7 communication system is:

  • able to be integrated into a standard FACP of the building.
  • able to be installed in several configurations throughout various building type applications.
  • able to be readily used and maintained in varying conditions by operating property owners.
  • able to be integrated in to the central monitoring station communication system through GSM and PSTN
  • able to be scalable for future expansion
